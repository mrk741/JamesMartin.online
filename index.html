How It Works

A Simple4-Step Process

Getting your audio transcribed has never been easier. Follow these simple steps and receive your polished documents in no time.

1

Submit Your Files

Upload your audio or video files through our secure portal or email them directly. We support all major formats including MP3, WAV, MP4, and more.

2

Expert Transcription

Your files are assigned to a specialist transcriber with expertise in your specific field. They carefully listen and transcribe every word with precision.

3

Quality Assurance

Every transcript goes through a rigorous two-step review process. Our editors check for accuracy, formatting, spelling, and industry-specific terminology.

4

Receive & Review

Download your completed transcript in your preferred format. We offer free revisions within 30 days to ensure your complete satisfaction.
